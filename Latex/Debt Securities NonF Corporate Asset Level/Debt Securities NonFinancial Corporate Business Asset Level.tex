Debt securities is a measure provided by the Board of Governor of the Federal Reserve System U.S. and is freely available on Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED). It includes 

\begin{itemize}
	\item open market paper
	\item Treasury securities
	\item agency- and GSE-backed securities
	\item municipal securities
	\item corporate and foreign bonds
\end{itemize}

The nonfinancial business sector equals the sum of two sectors: nonfinancial corporate business and nonfinancial
noncorporate business.
